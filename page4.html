What is an Astrology Reading
Astrology is the study of the stars, moons and planets, and how they influence the course of individual people's lives. It is believed that Astrology began and developed in the ancient Far East.  Some of these ancient types of astrology are still present to this day and are still performed all around the world.
Most people are familiar with astrology in terms of the Zodiac signs. The simplist form of Astrology is sun-sign astrology. These are the astrology readings you read in newspaper Horoscopes and because they are based only on the basic date of birth, they generally produce very generic results.
This form of date of birth Astrology is also known as Natal Astrology, or The Law of Beginnings.

Mundane Astrology is another form of Asterological Reading and generally refers to the forecasting of broad predictions about worldwide geological events, political events and the antics of the famous people.
A more personal Asterological reading will look deep into the details of birth times, dates and the planitary activity for those specific details. For example, two people born on the same day will probably have similar simple readings but, if they were also born in different years, times or days they will most definitely have different details in their readings.
Interrogatory Astrology is an even more indepth style of reading. This is a very specific readingwhich take into account of predictions for individual people and can address anything from a person's health or lifespan to mate compatibility and major life events.

Each person has a rising sign and a descending sign. The rising sign is the sun sign, or the one that most people are familiar with when asked for their Zodiac sign. For instance, if you were born on April 18, your rising sign would be Aries, but your descending sign, or moon sign, would be Taurus. A good astrology reading will take both into account.
The sun sign is actually the least telling about the two signs. Most people don't realize that their rising sign will probably tell more about the first impressions they give people than about their true self. Your rising sign will reveal some of your personality, but your descending sign, the moon sign, will reveal more about your true emotions and inner self.
A good astrology reading will place just as much emphasis on reading your moon sign as it does on your sun sign. The moon sign will tell the astrologer about a person's subconscious. Traits, emotions and talents that may be hidden to most will come to light here. A moon sign also will reveal some aspects of a person's physical health.
Other symbols depicted in astrology include those of luminaries, or other heavenly bodies. There are specific symbols for each of the following: the sun, moon, Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune and Pluto.
Another aspect of the astrology reading deals with the astrological houses. The astrology chart, which shows each of the twelve Zodiac signs, is also divided into twelve different houses. These astrological houses will reveal even more details about a person's life, such as details about their money and investments, travel and education, childhood memories, relationships, family and career.
Planetary Aspects is another important part in an astrology reading. Planetary Aspects refers to the mathematical angles of each planet in relationship to another. The aspects are divided into two sub categories, major and minor. These angles can reveal hidden energies, abilities, opportunities and skills.

There are many different elements to an astrology reading that should be learned and understood before a psychic, medium or astrologer can give an accurate reading. Astrology is a detailed science with a rich history in both predicting and changing the course of human lives.


All astrology reading begins with an examination of a persons birth chart. This birth chart will have details specific to the persons exact date of birth and shows specific symbols that reveal things about that person's present and future.
In each Asterological reading a birth date symbol is represented by a hieroglyph letter and also a simply drawn symbol, such as the lion for Leo and the fish for Pisces.
